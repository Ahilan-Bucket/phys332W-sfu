%==============================================================================
\subsection*{Summary}
%==============================================================================

Sessions 3--4 transitioned from passive observations (beads, fixed cells) to exploring active biological transport. The key discovery was \textbf{onion cell cytoplasmic streaming} with MSD exponent $\alpha = 1.66 \pm 0.05$, clearly distinguishing directed intracellular transport from Brownian diffusion. The calibration error from Session 2 ($5\times$ overestimate) was definitively resolved by consistent measurements across Sessions 1, 3, and 4 (all within 68.4--68.7~nm/px).

\textbf{Not completed:} \textit{E.\ coli} imaging (culture unavailable), bead diffusion verification (deferred to Sessions 5--6).

\textbf{Selected project for Sessions 5--6:} Bead Diffusion (varied viscosities and sizes) + yeast cell population study.
