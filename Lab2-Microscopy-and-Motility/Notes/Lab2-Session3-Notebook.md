8.3 Yeast Motion Observations
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Motion observed in videos:
- Cells VIBRATE (Brownian motion visible)
- NO DIRECTED motion observed
- Yeast cells do not actively swim

This is expected: Yeast (S. cerevisiae) is non-motile.
The vibration is thermal Brownian motion, not self-propulsion.
